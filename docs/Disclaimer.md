# Disclaimer

**This project is not legal or tax advice and has not been reviewed by a lawyer.**

It is an informational tool that encodes publicly available Thai statutory rules
(Civil and Commercial Code; National Health Act B.E. 2550; Inheritance Tax Act
B.E. 2558) to help organize estate planning and produce **draft** documents.

- Using it creates **no attorney–client relationship**.
- Every generated document is a **draft**. Do not sign it, or have anyone witness
  it, until a **licensed Thai probate/estate lawyer** has reviewed it.
- Laws and tax thresholds change. Figures and clauses here may be out of date; you
  are responsible for verifying current law.
- Advice is generated from the answers provided and cannot account for the full
  facts of any real situation.
- Use at your own discretion and risk.

This mirrors the acknowledgment gate in the web app and the header banner on every
page. See [[Home]] to navigate the rest of the docs.
