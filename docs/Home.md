# Estate Planning — Thailand · Home

Map of Content for this project. It powers a login-gated web app and a CLI that
give Thai-law estate-planning advice and generate draft documents (last will,
living will, medical POA, asset inventory) in English, Thai, or dual language.

> **Not legal advice.** Everything here is informational and unreviewed by a
> lawyer. See [[Disclaimer]] and verify with a licensed Thai probate/estate lawyer
> before relying on anything.
